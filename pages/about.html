Why This Exists

The Decision Nobody Was Helping You Make

Online education transformed how India learns. Working professionals could upskill without quitting. Learners in Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities gained access they never had before. The potential was real. But so was the noise.

Admissions became driven by aggressive sales calls, not student alignment. Standard search engines showed paid ad outcomes, and rankings lacked clear methodology. We saw a different opportunity: to build the honest, data-driven platform we wish had existed when we were making our own education decisions.
