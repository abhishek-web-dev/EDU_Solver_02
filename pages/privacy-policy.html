Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 29, 2026. Understand how we secure, process, and protect your educational counseling metrics.

1. Data Collection Framework

We collect personal credentials including name, email address, mobile number, target program categories, and preferred consultation times solely to map your profile to our certified Career Development Advisors (CDAs). All submissions represent direct opt-ins.

2. UGC-DEB Direct Directives Parity

EduSolver aggregates verified fees, regulatory rankings, and WES accreditation scores directly from institutional registries. We do not represent universities nor charge mediators' fees. Your tuition is paid directly into authorized university accounts.

3. Information Protection Compliance

We employ state-of-the-art secure socket layers (SSL) and digital system firewalls to ensure that aspirant credentials remain completely confidential. We do not sell, rent, or lease student contact indices to external marketing networks.

4. Cookies & Trackers

Our platform utilizes light cookie markers to remember target stream configurations, program checklist selections, and active checkbox parameters within our Side-by-Side Comparison matrices.
