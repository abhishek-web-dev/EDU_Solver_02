Refund & Cancellation Policy

Understand admissions cancellation timelines, tuition payment processing, and refund parameters in cooperation with target universities.

Under formal regulatory frameworks, students possess the right to cancel online admissions registration. Cancellations submitted prior to university LMS account activation receive a full tuition refund, minus nominal application verification processing charges.

Approved refunds are processed in cooperation with target university accounting wings. Valid refund balances are remitted directly to your bank account within 14-30 working days from the formal date of admissions cancel approvals.

Cancellation of admissions secured through zero-cost EMI plans requires formal bank approvals. Payer profiles must verify cancel clearances with financial partners to avoid monthly credit debit processing logs.
